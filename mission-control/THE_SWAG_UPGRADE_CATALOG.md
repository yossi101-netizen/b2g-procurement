## 0. THE MODULARITY ENGINE (how mix-and-match works)

Every SKU is classified into one of three modular roles so an HR Director can assemble any kit without designer help:

| Role | Definition | Price weight | Examples |
|------|-----------|-------------|----------|
| **ANCHOR** | The 1 statement hero that defines the kit's perceived value | High | Laptop folio, weekender, desk pad |
| **CONNECTOR** | Mid-size functional item that bridges anchor to accents | Medium | Tech organizer, dopp kit, A5 journal cover |
| **ACCENT** | Small, low-cost, high-touch personal item; the "delight" filler | Low | Card holder, key fob, luggage tag, cable wrap |

**Kit formula:** `1 ANCHOR + 1–2 CONNECTORS + 2–3 ACCENTS` = a complete, premium-feeling kit at a controllable price band.

**Shared design system (mandatory for modularity):**
- One leather (full-grain), 5 colorways: **Black, Cognac, Dark Brown, Charcoal, Deep Navy.**
- One stitch palette, one edge-paint set, one hardware finish (matte gunmetal or antique brass — pick ONE per kit).
- One logo placement grid → any item can be customized identically → kits always look like a coordinated set.

## 3. MATERIAL ALIGNMENT — why every item is a full-grain natural fit

- **All items are flat-panel or simple-construction goods** — sleeves, wraps, trays, holders — the categories where full-grain leather's thickness, patina, and edge-paint finish read as *most* premium and where cutting/stitching is straightforward for the Bangalore line.
- **No item requires molded, injection, or rigid-formed components** beyond a wrapped rigid core (pen holder) or a snap-fold structure (tray) — both standard leather-goods techniques.
- **Crazy Horse / full-grain ages into patina**, which we position as a feature ("gets better with use") vs. competitors' PU/plastic that degrades — directly attacks the "Welcome Kit Crisis" pain point.
- **5-colorway discipline** keeps hide sourcing simple and guarantees kits look coordinated.

## 4. CUSTOMIZATION CONSTRAINT — locked to local standard processes

Every SKU above is customizable using ONLY the three confirmed Bangalore/Chennai capabilities. **No exotic/proprietary machinery anywhere in the catalog.**

| Method | Best for | Look | Per-unit add (est.) |
|--------|----------|------|---------------------|
| **Blind deboss** | Subtle corporate logo on large flat areas (folio, desk mat, tray) | Tone-on-tone, understated luxury | $0.3–0.8 |
| **Foil stamp (gold / blind)** | High-contrast logo or monogram on smaller premium items (passport, journal) | Bold, prestige | $0.5–1.0 |
| **Laser engrave** | Initials/personalization, small accents (key fob, bookmark, card holder) | Precise, fast, no tooling | $0.3–0.7 |

**Rule of placement:** one logo zone per item, on the design-system grid, so any mix of items engraves identically and the kit reads as a coordinated set.

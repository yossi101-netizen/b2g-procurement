## The Pattern

Notice something? Every role uses the same 3 tracks, just in different combinations:

- **Content track** = when you need to publish or communicate externally
- **Sales track** = when you need to persuade someone to say yes
- **Thinking track** = when you need to analyze, summarize, or decide

The skills and workflows handle the specifics. You just pick the right combination for what you need today.

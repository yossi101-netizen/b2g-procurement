# Project Brief

## Who We Are
KritiKaal.com is a Managed Manufacturing service based in India, specializing in leather goods for international small and medium-sized businesses (SMBs).
We act as an end-to-end operational gateway to India — combining rigorous local oversight with international standards.

## What We Manufacture
Capabilities spanning bags, belts, SLGs (small leather goods), and custom brand developments, sourced across India's specialised leather-manufacturing clusters.

## What Problem We Solve
International businesses that want to manufacture leather goods in India face complexity, opacity, and risk: finding reliable suppliers, maintaining quality control, and navigating cross-border logistics — all without local presence or supply chain expertise.
We eliminate that problem by owning the entire process on their behalf.

## What Makes Us Different
- We are a managed, active operational partner — not a passive marketplace or directory like Alibaba.
- End-to-end ownership: supplier vetting, QC, production oversight, and international shipping — all under one roof.
- Local eyes on the ground in India, held to international standards.
- We operate asset-light (no factory ownership) but with deep embedded QA inside partner factories.
- We serve SMBs who lack the resources to build their own supply chain team in India.

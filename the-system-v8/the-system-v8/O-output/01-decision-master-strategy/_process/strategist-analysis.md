## Options Evaluated

### Option A: Create Full MASTER Document Now

**What:** A comprehensive reference document synthesizing everything known about KritiKaal — business model, revenue mechanics, competitive positioning, operational model, go-to-market, key numbers. Saved in B-brain/ as the AI's permanent business bible.

**Upside:**
- All future agents start with full business context from day 1
- No re-explaining the same concepts in every session
- sample-03 (44KB research doc) gets distilled into a reusable, searchable reference
- Competitive intelligence (Fashinza, Geniemode, Zetwerk) is permanently encoded
- Every future output is automatically more specific and relevant

**Downside:**
- Some sections may need updating as the business evolves
- Estimated 2-3 hours to build well (mitigated: we have all the source material now)

**Resource cost:** One session. No ongoing cost beyond quarterly review.
**Timeline:** Ready today. Value begins immediately.
**Confidence:** High — source material is sufficient.

---

### Option B: Build a Skeleton Now, Fill Incrementally

**What:** Create a lightweight MASTER file with section headers and placeholders. Agents fill gaps as they discover them over time.

**Upside:**
- Lower initial effort
- Avoids locking in anything premature

**Downside:**
- Agents operate with partial context for weeks
- "Incremental filling" rarely happens in practice — placeholders stay empty
- Every session is degraded until it's complete
- Estimated delay to full utility: 4-8 weeks

**Resource cost:** Lower upfront, but pays a tax on every session in between.
**Timeline:** "Ready" day 1 but useful only at 60-70% capacity until complete.
**Confidence:** Low — this approach tends to stay a skeleton forever.

---

### Option C: Do Nothing

**What:** Continue without a central business reference document. Each session sets its own context.

**What happens:** Every agent prompt requires re-explaining the business model, target markets, competitive positioning, and key numbers. The 44KB research document (sample-03) never gets encoded. Strategic decisions are made with inconsistent context.

**Cost of inaction:**
- Every future research session: 15-30 minutes of context-setting overhead
- Over 20 sessions: 5-10 hours of wasted setup
- Risk of inconsistent analysis across different sessions
- Competitive intelligence from sample-03 sits unused in a file nobody reads

**Verdict:** Unacceptable. The entire ABC-TOM system is built to compound knowledge. A missing MASTER file is a leak at the foundation.

# Thinking Samples

Add 3 documents you've written. These teach the system how you think, organize information, and communicate decisions.

**What works well:**
- Meeting summaries you wrote
- Emails about strategic decisions you sent to your team or co-founders
- User interview summaries
- PRDs or product briefs
- Stakeholder updates
- Project status reports
- Board reports or investor updates
- Quarterly reviews or strategy memos
- Team performance reviews or budget proposals
- Any document where you organized your thinking in writing

**What to avoid:**
- Documents someone else wrote (the system needs YOUR thinking style)
- Raw transcripts (those are INPUT for the system, not samples)
- Templates without your actual content

**How to choose:** Pick 3 documents that show how YOU structure information. The system will learn: Do you use bullet points or paragraphs? Do you lead with the conclusion or build up to it? Do you include action items? How much detail do you give? That's what it needs.

Paste into `sample-01.md`, `sample-02.md`, `sample-03.md`.

Hebrew or English. Either works.

# Sales Samples

Add 3 emails or messages you've sent to clients. These teach the system how you sell, propose, and communicate with clients.

**What works well:**
- Proposals you sent to potential clients
- Follow-up emails after meetings
- Offer emails with pricing or packages
- Sales emails that actually closed deals
- Any client-facing message where you were "selling" something

**What to avoid:**
- Auto-generated templates (Mailchimp, HubSpot templates)
- Emails someone else wrote for you
- Internal emails to your team (those belong in the Thinking track)

**How to choose:** Pick 3 emails where you were at your best. The ones where you nailed the tone, the client said yes, or you felt "I wrote this well." That's the voice the system needs to learn.

Paste into `sample-01.md`, `sample-02.md`, `sample-03.md`.

Hebrew or English. Either works.

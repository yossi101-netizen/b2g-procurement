# Content Samples

Add 3 pieces of content you've written. These teach the system your voice.

**What works well:**
- LinkedIn posts, articles, blog posts, newsletter issues, case studies
- Pieces where you think "this sounds like me"
- A mix of topics is fine (the system learns your VOICE, not your topics)

**What to avoid:**
- Content someone else wrote for you
- AI-generated content (the system needs YOUR voice, not ChatGPT's)
- Templates you filled in without changing the language

**How to choose:** Pick 3 pieces you're proud of. If someone read them without your name, would they know it's you? Those are the right ones.

Paste into `sample-01.md`, `sample-02.md`, `sample-03.md`.

Hebrew or English. Either works.

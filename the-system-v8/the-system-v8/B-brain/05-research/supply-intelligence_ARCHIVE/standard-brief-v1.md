## GOLDEN SAMPLE EVALUATION (for your information)

Once samples are received, they will be evaluated against these 8 criteria:

1. Leather thickness (spec: 1.2–1.4mm) — physically measured
2. Stitching density (spec: 8–10 SPI saddle stitch) — counted
3. Hardware security — pull test on D-rings, zip, magnetic snap
4. Edge finishing — burnished or painted, no raw edges
5. Lining attachment — no puckering, no glue bleed
6. Base stud positioning — 4 studs, symmetric, flush
7. Handle attachment — stress test, 5kg load
8. Overall silhouette — vs. technical specification, ±5mm tolerance

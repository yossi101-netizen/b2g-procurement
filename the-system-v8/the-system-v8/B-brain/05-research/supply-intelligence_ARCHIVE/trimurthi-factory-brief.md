2. **35+ years of experience** (if accurately reported — predates 2015 registration, possibly a family business reorganization).
3. **Chennai location.** Tamil Nadu is a major finished leather goods export hub (alongside Agra and Kanpur). Proximity to port (Chennai Port) reduces logistics lead times.
4. **Full product range.** Covers all major KritiKaal categories (bags, wallets, belts, accessories).
5. **Export-oriented.** Listed business type is "Exporter."

## Red Flags

1. **MOQ of 1–5 pieces.** This is micro-production territory. Either Trimurthi is very small-scale, or their TradeIndia listing is aspirational rather than operational. A legitimate export manufacturer typically has MOQs of 50–200 units minimum. This needs direct verification.
2. **Rs.2,000–3,000 ex-factory price.** At $24–36 per finished bag, this is at the bottom of the market. KritiKaal's AQL 2.5 standard and premium positioning may not be compatible with this price point. Risk: race-to-bottom on quality.
3. **Quality page under construction.** Not acceptable for a prospective partner. Every KritiKaal partner must have verifiable QC documentation.
4. **No BSCI, SA8000, or ISO 9001.** European buyers (KritiKaal's Tier 1 ICP) routinely require these alongside LWG. Gap in certification stack.
5. **Website maintenance.** Multiple broken pages (404 on company page, under-construction quality page). Signals either limited digital presence investment or organizational distraction.
6. **Company page 404.** Can't verify corporate history, client list, or export market claims.

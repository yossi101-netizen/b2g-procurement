## Critical Geographic Finding

**The Calcutta Leather Complex (Bantala), Kolkata is the primary cluster.**

The Kolkata Leather Complex at Karaidanga, Bantala is Asia's largest integrated leather hub — 1,100 acres, ~500 manufacturing units, 20 km from Kolkata CBD. It concentrates export-oriented, compliance-ready finished goods factories in a single infrastructure-supported zone with a Common Effluent Treatment Plant (CETP), dedicated power supply, and West Bengal government-backed export incentives.

The majority of the highest-scoring candidates in this sweep are located here:
- Dugros Leather: Zone 8, Plot 622, Calcutta Leather Complex
- Om Leather Art Deco: Plot 1641, Zone 9, Calcutta Leather Complex
- Patranabis Leather Industries: Zone 1, Plot 18, Calcutta Leather Complex

**Strategic implication:** The playbook's "Kanpur first" assumption was CORRECTED by the research. Kanpur dominates raw hide / tannery production (buffalo and cow hides). The Calcutta Leather Complex dominates export-grade FINISHED GOODS manufacturing. KritiKaal needs finished goods factories. The search should remain Kolkata-primary, with Delhi/Noida as secondary (for premium SME operations), and Kanpur as the tannery intelligence layer (raw material pricing, not finished goods production).

## Strategic Structural Finding: The Kolkata Advantage

The Calcutta Leather Complex (Bantala) is the correct primary hunting ground, not Kanpur. Reason:

| Factor | Kanpur | Kolkata (CLC Bantala) |
|--------|--------|----------------------|
| Specialisation | Tanneries + raw hide | Finished goods manufacturing |
| Compliance density | Growing (UP 2025 policy) | Established (500 units, CETP, LWG presence) |
| Export orientation | Mixed | High (government-backed complex, STAR Export Houses) |
| Relevance to KritiKaal | Raw material pricing intel | **PRODUCTION PARTNERS** |
| Infrastructure | Variable | Government-planned complex |

**Kanpur is where leather originates. Kolkata CLC is where it becomes the product our clients need.**

Kanpur remains valuable as: (a) raw material pricing intelligence (already in B-brain), and (b) a secondary Tier 2 factory location for specific substrates (buffalo leather specialty).

## THE ABSOLUTE CRITERIA RULE

> No factory advances on geography alone. Every factory — Track A or Track B — must satisfy ALL four gates before receiving the Standard Brief or any client referral.

| Gate | Requirement | Disqualifier |
|------|-------------|--------------|
| G1 — Certification Floor | LWG Leather Working Group (any rating) OR BSCI/SA8000/SMETA (social audit) | Uncertified factories are eliminated regardless of trust signals |
| G2 — Category Match | Bags and accessories as primary or confirmed secondary product line | Footwear-only or garment-only factories are eliminated |
| G3 — Export Orientation | Active exports to EU, UK, North America, or Middle East | Domestic-only suppliers cannot meet client compliance expectations |
| G4 — Operational Maturity | Minimum 5 years operating, minimum 50 workers | Scale and survival threshold for production-grade supply |

**Geography note:** If a factory meets ALL four gates AND is in Tamil Nadu, the geography is a strategic advantage — not a criterion. The Founder's physical presence delivers 2–5 day sampling vs. 14-day DHL, relationship pricing, floor inspection capability, and trust networks that cannot be purchased. This is not a favour to TN factories — it is a compounding return on criteria that are already met.

## 21-DAY TEST PROTOCOL

**Test Trigger:** Standard Brief v1.0 dispatched simultaneously to all advancing factories
**Track A Delivery:** Physical hand-delivery by Founder during factory visits (Chennai/TN circuit)
**Track B Delivery:** Cold email outreach (standard professional introduction + brief attachment)

**Evaluation Variables (6):**

| Variable | Track A Benchmark | Track B Benchmark | Weight |
|----------|------------------|------------------|--------|
| 1. Response speed | Within 24 hours (physical meeting = immediate) | Within 72 hours | High |
| 2. Quote accuracy | All line items addressed | All line items addressed | High |
| 3. Price (FOB 100 + 500 units) | Within KritiKaal target range | Within KritiKaal target range | High |
| 4. Lead time (sample + production) | Sample ≤ 7 days (TN proximity) | Sample ≤ 21 days | Medium |
| 5. Certification documents | Unsolicited disclosure preferred | Unsolicited disclosure preferred | High |
| 6. Golden sample quality | AQL 2.5 pass (8-criteria eval) | AQL 2.5 pass (8-criteria eval) | Critical |

**Golden Sample AQL 2.5 — 8 Criteria:**
1. Leather thickness (spec: 1.2–1.4mm) — measured
2. Stitching density (spec: 8–10 SPI saddle stitch)
3. Hardware security (D-rings, zip, magnetic snap — pull test)
4. Edge finishing (burnished or painted — no raw edges)
5. Lining attachment (no puckering, no glue bleed)
6. Base stud positioning (4 studs, symmetric, flush)
7. Handle attachment (stress test — 5kg load)
8. Overall silhouette (vs. technical drawing — ±5mm tolerance)

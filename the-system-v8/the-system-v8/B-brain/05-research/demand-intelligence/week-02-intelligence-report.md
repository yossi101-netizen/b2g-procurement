## The Single Most Important Insight From Week 2

**The ICP is not one segment — it is three plays with different pitches.**

Play 1 (China Plus One) requires a risk-mitigation message: *"Your Changshu factory is one audit failure away from a retail delisting. Here is your India alternative."*

Play 2 (India Upgrade) requires a capability message: *"You are already in India. The question is whether your current India partner can scale to your growth rate and survive an LWG audit. Ours can."*

Play 3 (Europe Cost Pressure) requires a financial message: *"Your Spanish artisan costs are increasing 8-12% per year. Here is what the same quality looks like at Kanpur pricing."*

The outreach narrative, the decision-maker profile, and the urgency level are different for each play. Week 3's dossier work must categorise each target by play before drafting any outreach.

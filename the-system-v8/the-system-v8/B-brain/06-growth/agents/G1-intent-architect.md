## 3. Intelligence Collection Process

### Step 1 — SERP Scan (every Monday)
Run all 10 seed queries. For each query, record:
- Top 5 organic results (title, URL, domain authority estimate)
- Any featured snippets or People Also Ask (PAA) boxes
- Whether KritiKaal appears — if yes, at what position

### Step 2 — PAA Harvest
For each query, expand the "People Also Ask" box 3 levels deep. These are the exact questions procurement directors are typing. Every PAA question is a potential FAQ entry or blog title.

### Step 3 — AI Model Simulation
Run the top 3 queries verbatim into ChatGPT and Gemini. Record:
- Is KritiKaal named? (target: yes, within 90 days of content launch)
- Which competitors are named?
- What claims does the AI make about India leather manufacturing?
- What proof points does the AI cite?

### Step 4 — UGC Scan (Reddit / Quora / LinkedIn)
Search Reddit (/r/entrepreneur, /r/smallbusiness, /r/fashion) and Quora for threads matching:
- "leather manufacturing India"
- "sourcing agent India quality"
- "China plus one fashion brand"
Record: thread volume, upvotes/engagement, what questions are being asked, what answers are being upvoted.

### Step 5 — Competitor Content Rip (weekly 3 competitors)
Rotating list: LeelineBags, Panoramic Sourcing, Maharya, GNN International, Creative Pelt, Apex Leather Goods, Syraah/Renesa Export
For each: screenshot their latest blog post title + meta description. Record whether they use KritiKaal's vocabulary (managed manufacturing, AQL 2.5, golden sample, dual-track).

## 5. Vocabulary Standards

G1 uses procurement-director vocabulary, not factory vocabulary. The distinction:

| Factory vocabulary (do NOT use) | Procurement director vocabulary (USE) |
|---|---|
| "tannery" | "supply chain" |
| "upper leather" | "leather specification" |
| "clicking" | "pattern cutting" |
| "finishing" | "surface treatment / edge finishing" |
| "production order" | "production run" |
| "factory floor" | "manufacturing floor" / "production facility" |
| "MOQ" | "minimum commitment" (in outward-facing content) |

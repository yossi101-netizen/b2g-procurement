## Section 3 — CRITICAL BUGS (fix before any SEO/GEO investment)

### BUG 1 — LIVE, HIGH PRIORITY: Comparison Table Headers SWAPPED (why-india page)
**Page:** kritikaal.com/why-india
**Section:** "Managed Manufacturing VS Sourcing Agencies"
**Problem:** The column headers are inverted. "Managed Manufacturing" currently shows:
- No Oversight
- Hidden Kickbacks
- Ethical Blind Spots

And "Sourcing Agencies" currently shows:
- Direct Control
- Full Transparency
- Ethical Audits

**This is the exact opposite of the intended message.** A procurement director reading this page is being told that KritiKaal's "Managed Manufacturing" model has no oversight and hidden kickbacks. This is a live positioning disaster.

**Fix:** Swap the two column header labels, or swap the content blocks. The positive attributes belong to "KritiKaal / Managed Manufacturing." The negative attributes belong to "Traditional Sourcing Agencies."

**Priority:** Fix this before any paid traffic, any outreach, and any AI citation — if ChatGPT or Gemini crawls this page, it will extract the inverted comparison.

---

### BUG 2 — POSITIONING CONFLICT: MOQ 300–500 on Products Page
**Page:** kritikaal.com/products
**Problem:** Every product card displays "Min. Order Quantity: 300 Units / 500 Units." Phase 1 supply strategy established 100-unit MOQ as the entry point for anchor-tier clients (KK-TB-001 brief uses 100-unit FOB pricing as the baseline test).
**Implication:** A procurement director from a mid-size heritage brand (Aspinal, Scaramanga tier) who lands on the products page sees 300-unit MOQ and may disqualify KritiKaal without booking a call.
**Options:**
- A) Remove the MOQ from product cards entirely (discovery-only, discuss on call) — recommended for B2B positioning
- B) Change to "MOQ from 100 units — varies by product" 
- C) Keep as-is and accept that it filters out sub-300 prospects

**Founder decision required.**

---

### BUG 3 — UX ISSUE: Book a Call Shows Israel Timezone as Default
**Page:** kritikaal.com/bookacall
**Problem:** The scheduling calendar defaults to "Israel — Israel Time (UTC+2:00)." KritiKaal's target market is UK/EU procurement directors. A UK buyer seeing "Israel Time" as the default:
1. Must manually change timezone (friction)
2. May question whether this is a UK company (trust issue)
**Fix:** Set default timezone to "United Kingdom — GMT/BST (UTC+0:00 / UTC+1:00)."

---

### BUG 4 — BLACKLIST VIOLATION: "World-Class Quality" on Live Site
**Page:** kritikaal.com/why-india
**Text:** "Skilled Artisans — Generational craftsmanship meets modern training. 👤 World-Class Quality"
**Problem:** "World-Class" is on the Voice DNA blacklist. It is a hollow claim that undermines the specificity mandate.
**Fix:** Replace with a specific proof point. Suggested replacement:
> *"Artisan clusters in Chennai, Kolkata, Kanpur, and Agra — serving European export standards for 40+ years."*

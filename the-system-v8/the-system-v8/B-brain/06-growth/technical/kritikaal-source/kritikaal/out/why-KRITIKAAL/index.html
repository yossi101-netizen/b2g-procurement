    Our Process

    Manufacturing Controls

    A clear control system from approved sample to export-ready delivery.

    LOCK

    LOCKED

    Spec Lock

    Materials, dimensions, and construction details are locked before production starts.

    LOCKED

    Material Confirmation

    Leather type, thickness, and finishes are confirmed against the approved sample.

    LOCKED

    Production Brief

    Factory receives a controlled spec pack and cannot change details without approval.

    CONTROL

    IN-PROCESS

    In-Process QC

    We inspect stitching, edge paint, and finishing during production - not after.

    IN-PROCESS

    Daily Progress Tracking

    Daily updates track output and deviations against the agreed timeline.

    IN-PROCESS

    Issue Escalation

    If something deviates, we correct it internally before it becomes a batch-level failure.

    DELIVER

    DELIVERED

    Final QC Gate

    No shipment is released without a final pass against approved standards.

    DELIVERED

    Export-Ready Packing

    Goods are packed, labeled, and prepared to ship based on export requirements.

    DELIVERED

    Documentation & Handoff

    We prepare documents and hand off to the freight forwarder (yours or ours).

    Financial Safety

    Your Money Stays Safe

    The Escrow Model

    1. Client

    Initiates payment for order. Funds leave your account.

    2. Escrow Hold

    Funds are secured in a neutral holding account. The factory cannot touch them yet.

    3. QC Inspection

    Production completes. KRITIKAAL performs final quality audit.

    4. Release

    Funds released to factory ONLY after KRITIKAAL approves QC.

    🛡️

    Performance-Based Payment

    The factory doesn't get paid until the product meets your exact Golden Sample specs.

    About KRITIKAAL

    KRITIKAAL was founded to solve a problem most brands only discover after it costs them real money.

    Over the years, we've worked extensively with overseas suppliers - including deep, hands-on experience with manufacturers in China. That experience exposed two realities that many brands underestimate until it's too late.

    Based in India
    Serving Global Brands

    Bringing decades of experience in leather manufacturing and a commitment to quality craftsmanship.

    Founded by

    Yossi Daniel

    Founder & CEO

    China is no longer the low-cost
    manufacturing solution it once was

    For more than a decade, China was the default choice for cost-efficient manufacturing. That reality has changed.

    Over the last 10–15 years:

    3×Manufacturing wages increase in many industrial regions
    Compliance, environmental, and export costs risen significantly
    Priority given to smaller and mid-sized brands vs large buyers

    For many SMB brands today, China is no longer cheaper - only more complex and less forgiving.

    India Presents a Strategic Alternative

    Lower labor costs

    For skilled leather craftsmanship

    Raw material access

    Strong domestic access to quality materials

    Export ecosystem

    Growing, export-oriented manufacturing

    Flexibility

    Greater flexibility for small and mid-scale runs

    But cost alone is not the real advantage.

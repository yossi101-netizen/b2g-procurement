### What KritiKaal Is

KritiKaal.com is a Managed Manufacturing service based in India, specializing exclusively in leather goods for international SMBs. We are an end-to-end operational partner — not a marketplace, not a broker, not an agent.

**The one-line version:** We are the supply chain team you don't have, embedded in India, working exclusively on your leather goods.

**The problem we solve:** International brands want to manufacture leather goods in India but face complexity, opacity, and risk — no local presence, no trusted factory relationships, unreliable QC, language gaps, and cross-border logistics they can't manage from abroad.

**What makes us different:** A Single Point of Accountability. One project owner on the ground. End-to-end ownership from raw hide to container loading. We don't connect you to a factory. We manage the factory on your behalf.

### What KritiKaal Is NOT

- Not a passive marketplace (Alibaba-model)
- Not a fast-fashion cheap middleman

Mid-sized fashion brands (SMEs), product managers, and designers in Europe, UK, Australia, and the US seeking a "China Plus One" manufacturing strategy. They want consistent, scalable manufacturing without building their own supply chain team in India. They've been burned before or are afraid of being burned.

### Supply-Side Intelligence: Kanpur Leather Pricing (March 2026)

### 5 Non-Negotiable Rules for Every Kanpur Order

| # | Rule | Why It Matters |
|---|------|----------------|
| 1 | **Always specify FULL GRAIN ONLY** | Split and split cracker look identical until touched. Say it in every order. |

### The Business Model

**Model type:** Asset-light Managed Manufacturing (no factory ownership)

KritiKaal does not own factories. It embeds its QA protocols, project management, and accountability layer into partner factories in India (primarily Bangalore, also Kanpur and Tamil Nadu for specific categories).

**Revenue model:** $5,000 average deal size. Path to $1M ARR = 200 deals/year (~17 per month). [TARGET]

**Financial protection for clients:** Escrow-based payments. Client funds are held by a neutral third party and released to the factory only after the pre-shipment AQL 2.5 inspection passes.

---

### The Operational Stack ("The Trust Stack")

Three layers of protection that no digital marketplace can replicate:

**Layer 1: Physical QA presence**
A KritiKaal QA inspector is physically present at the factory at three stages:
1. Pre-production: "Golden Sample" sign-off — a physically signed sample that is the binding quality reference for the entire run
2. Mid-line inspection (at 10–20% production completion): catches systematic errors before they multiply
3. Pre-shipment inspection: AQL 2.5 standard — 99/100 qualified units before release

**Layer 2: Financial accountability — The Double-Back Guarantee**
If a functional defect passes QC and reaches the client's warehouse: credit = 2x the unit value, not just a refund.

**Layer 3: IP protection — Split Manufacturing**
For premium or proprietary designs, production is split across multiple partner facilities. No single factory sees the complete design. NNN agreements enforced with all partners.

### Regulatory and Compliance Moat

**REACH compliance:** EU chemical regulation banning Chromium VI and other harmful substances. KritiKaal audits tannery chemical chains and provides verified compliance documentation.

**LWG (Leather Working Group):** KritiKaal sources exclusively from LWG-certified tanneries. Note: In 2024–2025, the Bantala industrial zone in Kolkata had its central effluent treatment plant suspended, pulling LWG certification from dozens of tanneries overnight — disrupting supply chains of any platform that didn't track tannery certification at source level.

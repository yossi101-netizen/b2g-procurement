# Prompt: Learn My Communication & Decision-Making Style

Copy and paste this into your chat:

---

```
Read all the writing samples in B-brain/02-my-samples/ -- check ALL subfolders (01-content/, 02-sales/, 03-thinking/) and read whatever samples you find. I may have placed my samples in any of these folders.

Even if my samples are social posts or client emails (not strategy documents), you can learn how I think from the way I argue, persuade, and structure ideas.

Then:

1. Update C-core/voice-dna.md - add specific patterns you noticed about how I communicate and think (tone, structure, document preferences)

2. Update A-agents/analyst-agent.md - add a new section called "Owner's Document Style" with concrete patterns the analyst should follow when structuring documents for me

3. Update A-agents/strategist-agent.md - add a new section called "Owner's Decision Style" with concrete patterns the strategist should follow when presenting analysis to me

4. Update A-agents/chief-of-staff-agent.md - add a new section called "Owner's Brief Preferences" with how I like information presented (level of detail, risk tolerance, data vs intuition balance)

5. Update M-memory/learning-log.md - add an entry documenting what you learned about my style, with specific examples from my samples

Show me a summary of what you learned and what you updated.
```
